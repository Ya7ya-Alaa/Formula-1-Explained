About Aston Martin

Aston Martin Cognizant Formula One Team is a British Formula 1 team based in Silverstone, Northamptonshire. The team was rebranded from Racing Point to Aston Martin in 2021, marking a new era for the iconic British brand in motorsport. Known for its green livery and commitment to performance, Aston Martin aims to compete at the highest level in Formula 1.
