Ferrari

About Ferrari

Scuderia Ferrari is the racing team division of the Italian sports car manufacturer Ferrari. Founded in 1929, it is one of the oldest and most successful teams in Formula 1 history. The team has a rich legacy, with numerous Constructors' and Drivers' Championships to its name. Known for its iconic red cars, Ferrari is synonymous with speed, performance, and passion in motorsport.
