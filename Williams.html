Williams

About Williams

Williams Racing is a British Formula 1 team founded in 1977 by Sir Frank Williams and Patrick Head. Known for its rich history and engineering excellence, the team has won multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. Williams has been a significant force in F1, with legendary drivers like Nigel Mansell, Alain Prost, and Damon Hill contributing to its success.

Drivers

As of the 2025 season, Williams fields two talented drivers:

  • Alex Albon - A promising driver with a strong performance record. Nationality: Thai
  • Carlos Sainz - A historic driver continuing his legacy in Formula 1. Nationality: Spanish

Team Principal

The team principal for Williams in the 2025 season is James Vowles, who has been pivotal in leading the team through recent challenges and striving for competitive performance.

Achievements

  • 9 Constructors' Championships (1980, 1981, 1986, 1987, 1992, 1993, 1994, 1996, 1997)
  • 7 Drivers' Championships
  • Over 100 Grand Prix victories
  • Renowned for technical innovation and engineering excellence

Williams has a storied history in Formula 1, with numerous championships and iconic moments that have shaped the sport.

Summary

Williams Racing is a team with a rich heritage in Formula 1, known for its engineering prowess and competitive spirit. With a focus on innovation and performance, Williams continues to strive for success in the ever-evolving world of motorsport.
