Red Bull

About Red Bull

Red Bull Racing is a Formula 1 team based in Milton Keynes, England. Established in 2005, the team has quickly risen to prominence in the sport, winning multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. Known for its innovative approach and strong performance, Red Bull Racing is a key player in Formula 1.

2025 Season Overview

For the 2025 season, Red Bull Racing has introduced a new car designed to compete at the highest level. The team aims to continue its success in the Constructors' Championship with a blend of experienced drivers and cutting-edge technology.

Drivers

Red Bull Racing's driver lineup for the 2025 season includes:

Team Principal

The team principal for Red Bull Racing in the 2025 season is Laueren Mekies, who has been appointed after Christian Horner's departure.

Achievements

Red Bull Racing has a remarkable record in Formula 1, with numerous achievements including:

  • 4 Constructors' Championships
  • 4 Drivers' Championships
  • Over 80 Grand Prix victories

Summary

Red Bull Racing continues to be a dominant force in Formula 1, with a commitment to excellence and a relentless pursuit of victory. The team's innovative spirit and competitive edge make it a formidable contender on the track, and its legacy in the sport is one of unparalleled success and achievement.
