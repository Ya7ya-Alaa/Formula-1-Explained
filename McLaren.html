About McLaren

McLaren Racing is a British Formula 1 team based in Woking, Surrey. Founded in 1963 by Bruce McLaren, the team has a rich history and is one of the most successful teams in Formula 1. Known for its innovative engineering and competitive spirit, McLaren has won multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships.
