About Mercedes

Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team is the racing team division of the German automotive manufacturer Mercedes-Benz. Established in 2010, the team has quickly become one of the most successful in Formula 1 history, winning multiple Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. Known for its innovative engineering and performance, Mercedes continues to set the standard in the sport.
